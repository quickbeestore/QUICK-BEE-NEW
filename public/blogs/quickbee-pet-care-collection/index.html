    Nutrition Guide: What Your Pet Needs

    Dogs Nutritional Requirements

  • Protein: 18-25% minimum (essential for growth and maintenance)
  • Fat: 5% minimum (for energy and nutrient absorption)
  • Fiber: 1-3% (for digestive health)
  • Vitamins & Minerals: Balanced for overall health
  • Hydration: Fresh water always available

    Cats Nutritional Requirements

  • Protein: 25% minimum (obligate carnivores)
  • Taurine: Essential amino acid for heart and eye health
  • Fat: Higher than dogs (for energy and nutrient absorption)
  • Moisture: High moisture food important for hydration
  • Vitamins & Minerals: Including taurine-rich meat

    Small Animals Nutritional Requirements

  • Hay: Primary food source
  • Pellets: Balanced nutrition supplement
  • Fresh vegetables: Daily nutrition and enrichment
  • Vitamin C: Critical for guinea pigs
  • Calcium & Phosphorus: Proper balance for bones
