    The Science Behind Perfect Bubble Tea

    Tea Brewing Mastery

Water temperature and steeping time are critical:
  • Green tea: 70-80°C, 2-3 minutes
  • Oolong tea: 85-95°C, 3-5 minutes
  • Black tea: 95-100°C, 3-5 minutes
  • Precise timing ensures optimal flavor extraction

    Pearl Perfection

Tapioca pearls require expert preparation:
  • Cooked from scratch daily
  • Soaked in brown sugar syrup for flavor
  • Chewy texture achieved through precise cooling
  • Topped with thin brown sugar film

    Milk Integration

Proper milk mixing creates smoothness:
  • Temperature equilibrium between tea and milk
  • Proportion balance (typically 3:1 tea to milk)
  • Gentle mixing prevents curdling
  • Visual appeal with distinct layers

Caring for Your Bubble Tea

Optimal Enjoyment Tips

  • Cold bubble tea: Best consumed within 30 minutes for optimal pearl texture
  • Hot bubble tea: Enjoy within 45 minutes while flavors are vibrant
  • Pearl texture: Pearls stay chewy for 1-2 hours; longer and they harden
  • Lid removal: Pop the seal to reveal the drink – it's part of the experience

    Storage Guidance

  • Don't refrigerate after delivery – enjoy fresh
  • If needed, store in refrigerator up to 4 hours
  • Pearls harden if stored; consume fresh for best texture
  • Never freeze unless specifically ordered frozen
