Storage & Freshness Tips

To maximize your QuickBee brownie experience:

- Room Temperature: Best enjoyed within 2-3 days of delivery

  • Refrigerator: Extends freshness up to 5 days
  • Freezer: Can be frozen for up to 2 weeks
  • Reheating: Microwave for 15-20 seconds for warm indulgence
  • Pro Tip: Pair with vanilla ice cream for an elevated dessert experience
