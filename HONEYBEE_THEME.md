# 🐝 QuickBee Honey Bee Theme - Complete Implementation

## Theme Overview
A comprehensive honey bee-themed e-commerce platform redesign featuring warm gold, brown, and black colors with animated bee motifs and honeycomb-inspired card designs.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Gold**: #FDB913 (Bright honey)
- **Secondary Gold**: #FFD700 (Rich gold)
- **Dark Brown**: #8B4513 (Saddle brown)
- **Dark Secondary**: #6B4423 (Deep brown)
- **Accent Black**: #000000 (Bee stripes)
- **Light**: #FFF8DC (Cornsilk background)

### 🎨 Design Elements
1. **Animated Bee Mascot** - Floating 🐝 with rotation animation
